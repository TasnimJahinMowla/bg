Our Services

Emergency Services Integration

Users can quickly access emergency services such as police, medical assistance, and fire department through the app.

Anonymous Incident Reporting

Users can report incidents of crime or suspicious activities they witness in their vicinity anonymously.

Geographical Alert System

When a user reports a violent crime or a missing person in a specific location, the app triggers a geographical alert system. This system sends out alerts to all users within the affected area, providing them with essential information and urging caution.

Criminal Database Alerts

The app can cross-reference reported incidents with a database of known criminals. If a violent criminal matching the description is spotted nearby, the app sends an immediate alert to nearby users, advising them to be cautious.
